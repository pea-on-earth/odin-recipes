Delicious and Classic Lasagna

Entertain your family and friends with this bellyhugging and familiar lasagna recipe. Simple ingredients shine here, and while there is quite a bit of prep, it will be worth it, and makes for great leftovers.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. sweet italian sausage
  • 3/4 lb. ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 tub ricotta
  • salt and pepper
  • 3/4 cup mozzerella and parmesean cheese

Steps

  1. cook sausage and beef until browned over medium heat. add in onions and garlic and herbs. simmer for 1 1/2 hours.
  2. boil lasanga noodes for 8-10 mins. drain and rinse with cold water. mix ricotta with egg and salt in mixing bowl.
  3. assemble lasagna in greased dish in layers, using noodles to separate.
  4. bake in preheated oven at 375 degrees for 25 minutes covered, then 25 minutes uncovered.
