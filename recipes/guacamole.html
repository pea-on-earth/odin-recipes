Guacamole

The Dip of Dreams

Ingredients

  • 2 avocados, peeled and pitted
  • 1 cup tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/4 cup onion, chopped
  • 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 jalepeno, chopped and seeded
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Mash avocados until creamy, with some chunks
  2. Add in tomatoes, onions, jalepenos, lemon juice, season with salt and pepper.
  3. Enjoy with your favorite chips!
